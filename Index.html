WHAT WE BELIEVE

They were greatly inspired by the saying, "Nothing is impossible." They wanted the school to be an instrument for including values of universal brotherhood and cultivation of virtues. The school's motto "Enter to learn and leave to serve" is in fact its guiding mission. It is to inspire the children and the young youth to think in terms of welfare of all people to whom they come in contact with directly or indirectly.
