Facts of the School

R.N International School is away from the din and bustle of the town. It is an English medium co-educational institution that prepares pupils for All India Secondary School Examination and All india School Certificate Examination conducted by C.B.S.E. New Delhi. The school is managed by the governing society registered under the Society Act of 1860 through a representative and influential board of management appointed by it.The school was founded in 1986. It has two wings, one at Naurangabad, consisting of classes from Nursery to I & other consisting of classes from II to XII at Moradabad Road, Dhampur with ultra-modern and gracious facilities.The School is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. Medium of instruction in all classes is English. However, Hindi is compulsory up to class X and Sanskrit is also taught from class VI to VIII.Students at PMS are encouraged to develop a keen sense of self-identity and positive self esteem. Diversity is valued highly and students develop a good understanding of their multi-cultural world. The school embodies the essential ingredients of global school, always striving to stay on the cutting edge of new and sound educational programmes. The school has a student body that excels in academic and extracurricular endeavours, staff committed to excellance and a supportive parent community.The School has built up a strong and young generation with sound body, a well trained mind with habits and accomplishments conducive to a purposeful, fuller and noble life to blossom into an integrated personality.This school is a dynamic platform for students, teachers and school to open up the magical world of learning, by integrating learning with technology. Teachers can communicate instantly with students and their parents.
